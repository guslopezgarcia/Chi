About

I make delivery predictable

I've spent 10+ years turning underperforming engineering organizations into disciplined, predictable delivery machines — first in retail at Falabella, now inside two of Latin America's largest banks.

At Falabella, I scaled Lean Value Tree portfolio planning across Chile, India, and Peru, and turned around an offshore/onshore payments hub into a unit with a 100% Sprint Goal execution streak for a full year.
